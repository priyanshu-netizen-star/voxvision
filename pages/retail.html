Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your store online with VOXVISION.

How many products can I list on the website?+
Starter plan supports up to 50 products. Growth plan up to 200 products. Elite plan is unlimited products with full category management, filtering, search, and inventory tracking functionality.
Which payment methods do you integrate?+
We integrate Razorpay which supports UPI, credit and debit cards, net banking, and EMI options. Customers can also choose Cash on Delivery if you offer it. All transactions are fully secure and PCI compliant.
Do you set up Google Shopping ads?+
Yes - our Elite plan includes Google Shopping campaign setup and management to show your products in Google search results with product images and prices, driving highly targeted buyer traffic directly to your product pages.
Can you help with product photography for social media?+
We provide detailed guidance and shot lists for product photography. Our social media team uses your product images to create professional-looking Instagram and Facebook content with proper lighting tips and composition guidelines that drive sales.
