FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your school website built by VOXVISION.

Can parents apply for admission directly on the website?+
Yes - all Growth and Elite plans include a complete online admission form with document upload, application tracking status, and automated WhatsApp and email confirmation messages sent instantly to parents after submission.
How do you handle fee payment integration?+
We integrate Razorpay to allow parents to pay term fees, activity fees, and registration fees directly online with UPI, cards, or net banking. Automatic payment receipts are generated and emailed to parents instantly after every successful payment.
What social media content do you create for schools?+
We create parent-friendly content including classroom activities and events, student achievement spotlights, teacher features, educational tips for parents, school infrastructure showcases, and targeted admission season campaigns with paid promotions.
When should we launch our school website to maximize admissions?+
We recommend launching 60-90 days before your admission season opens, combined with a Google Ads and Facebook campaign targeting parents in your catchment area. This gives maximum time to build awareness and attract quality enquiries before your competitors.
