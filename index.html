FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see yours here, just ask us.

How long does it take to build a website? +
Typically 7–14 working days for a standard website, and 3–6 weeks for complex builds with booking systems, e-commerce, or custom features. We always provide a clear timeline before we begin.
Do you provide hosting and domain registration? +
Yes! We offer hosting setup and domain registration as part of our packages. We work with premium hosting providers to ensure your website is fast, secure, and always online.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Absolutely! Website redesigns are one of our specialties. We'll audit your current site, identify what's holding it back, and build a modern, high-converting replacement while preserving your SEO history.
Do you manage Instagram and other social media? +
Yes, we offer complete social media management for Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and LinkedIn. This includes content creation, posting, community management, analytics reports, and paid advertising.
Can I accept online payments through my website? +
Yes! We integrate payment gateways like Razorpay, PayU, and Stripe. Whether it's for bookings, products, memberships, or services — we'll set up a smooth, secure payment experience.
Do you provide SEO services? +
Yes! All our websites include basic SEO setup. We also offer comprehensive SEO packages including keyword research, content optimization, link building, Google Business Profile optimization, and monthly ranking reports.
Can you manage Google and Meta Ad campaigns? +
Absolutely! We create, manage, and optimize Google Search, Display, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram ad campaigns. We focus on maximizing your ROI with data-driven targeting and creative strategy.
What industries do you specialize in? +
We have deep expertise in Gyms, Hotels, Restaurants, Pubs & Bars, Law Firms, Schools, Hospitals & Clinics, and Retail Shops. Each industry gets a customized strategy tailored to its specific customer journey and goals.
